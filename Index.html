About Asheel's Online Store

Asheel's is an online marketplace and logistics company which operates in markets of South Asia and Southeast Asia. It was founded in 2012 as an online fashion e-commerce marketplace in Pakistan by Ubaid Kursheed (Founder) & Hanzala (Co-Founder) at the based Rocket Internet. Asheel's Group operates e-commerce platforms and logistics service in Bangladesh, Myanmar, Nepal, Pakistan and Sri Lanka. Aqib Rathor is the current CEO of Asheel's Online Store.
